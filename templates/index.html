About This ML Project

EatsEncore provide recommendations that will enhance a meal at a given restaurant. This machine learning recommendation engine utilized proprietary data from two restaurants. The second restaurant helped prove our code so that we can confidently say that the algorithm may be applied to data from additional restaurants or mobile app orders and, ideally, increase purchase volume.

Our three-person team completed this after-work project in approximately 3 weeks.
